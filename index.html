FAQ

Your Questions Are Answered Here

How can I browse Kaivera products by category?

Simply navigate to the Products page from the main menu. You'll see clearly labeled categories such as Dresses, Shirts, Shoes, and Perfumes. Click on any category to filter and explore relevant items.

How do I add an item to my cart?

On any product page, click the “Add to Cart” or "Buy" button beneath the item. The cart icon in the navigation bar will update instantly, allowing you to review or edit your selected products anytime.

How do I view or edit my cart before purchasing?

Click the menu icon at the top right of the page to go to Cart Page. You’ll see a full summary of your selected items, along with options to increase quantity, remove items, or proceed to checkout.

Can I see detailed information about each product?

Click on any product image or name to open its dedicated product page, which displays the full description, high-resolution images, category tag, and price in USD.

Is the website mobile-friendly and fast to load?

Definitely! Kaivera website is built with a responsive design, ensuring smooth browsing and functionality across all devices—from phones to desktops. Transitions and animations are optimized for a luxury and seamless experience.

How do I navigate the Kaivera website?

You can easily move between pages using the top navigation bar. Click on Home, Products, Cart, or Contact to access the respective sections. Smooth transitions ensure a seamless luxury browsing experience.
